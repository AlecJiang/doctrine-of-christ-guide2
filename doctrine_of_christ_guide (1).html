The Doctrine of Christ: A Foundational Guide

Explore faith, repentance, baptism, the Holy Ghost, and enduring to the end with clarity and purpose.

Faith Repentance Baptism Holy Ghost Endure

Faith in Jesus Christ

Faith is a principle of action and power. It means trusting Jesus Christ, believing in His power to save, and acting according to His word.

Repentance

Repentance is a change of heart and behavior. It involves turning away from sin and seeking to follow Christ more fully.

Baptism by Proper Authority

Baptism is a covenant with God and must be performed by one holding the priesthood. It represents cleansing from sin and commitment to follow Jesus.

The Gift of the Holy Ghost

The Holy Ghost is a companion, comforter, and guide. After baptism, we are confirmed and invited to receive His constant presence.

Enduring to the End

Enduring to the end means faithfully keeping covenants, repenting daily, and pressing forward with hope in Christ throughout life.
